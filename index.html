SMALL IDEA, BIG DIFFERENCE

Smart Dives Portable Surface Supplied System, Aquarius Series, features a new improve frame with umbilical protection plate. The umbilical protection plate may seem just like a dot on a piece of paper. But here are few facts that would change your mind.

The umbilical protection plate;

  • Prevents hoses from kinks. Kinks cause discontinuity of air flow and communication that will compromise the diver’s safety below water.
  • Ensures evenly coiled umbilical prevents from any damage.
  • Made of stainless steel with air vents. The air vent allows the umbilical to dry after operation.

Continual improvement either small or big is a step towards perfection.
